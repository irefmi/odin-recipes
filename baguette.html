Baguette Pizza Recipe

Description

A baguette pizza is a quick and easy, open-faced meal or snack made by slicing a baguette (or similar French/Italian loaf) in half lengthwise and baking it with classic pizza ingredients. It provides a fast, satisfying alternative to traditional pizza, utilizing the bread as a pre-made, crispy crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce, or as needed
  • 2/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 ounces cooked and crumbled sausage
  • 2 ounces mini pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Split baguette in half lengthwise. Warm pizza sauce in a microwave-safe bowl until hot, about 45 seconds.
  3. Spread pizza sauce on baguette, sprinkle on cheese, and scatter sausage and pepperoni on top as desired. Place pizza onto a baking tray.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is golden, about 18 minutes. Optional step: turn on the ovenâ€™s broiler, set a rack 6 inches below the heating element, and broil the pizza for a deeper color, 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Cut pizza crosswise into slices. Serve and enjoy.
